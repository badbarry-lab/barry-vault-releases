## ⚡ Core Features
* **Military-Grade Encryption:** Your data never leaves your device in plain text. Everything is encrypted using AES-256 standard via your Master Key.
* **Offline-First Architecture:** No cloud hacks, no server breaches. Your vault data is stored securely on your native Windows hardware.
* **Modular Node System:** Organize passwords, environment variables, and secure notes in a highly visual, nested tree structure.
* **Matrix Graph Engine:** Visualize your entire vault structure through a high-performance interactive map.
* **Dynamic Persistence:** Powered by Rust and Tauri, ensuring lightweight performance (under 5MB) and rock-solid data preservation.

## 🔒 Security Guarantee
Barry Vault operates on a zero-knowledge proof model. We do not track, collect, or upload your master password or your vault contents. **If you lose your Master Key, your data cannot be recovered.**
